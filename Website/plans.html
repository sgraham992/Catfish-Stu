About Our Plans

  • At Catfish Stu we keep it simple. We only have three plans, each that provides three meals a week. No reason to add more decisions than necessary.

  • Our beginner plan is the most popular. Most people start with this plan and for many it is perfect. The meals are straight forward and easy to cook and the preparation and cook times are usually the shortest.

  • For the more adventurous chef, try the Intermediate Plan. There is a bit more time spent in preparation and cooking but talk about impressing your friends. These meals are sure to blow your dinner guests away with exciting and innovative recipes to challenge the real foodies.

  • Finally the Kid Friendly meal plan. This plan is similar to the Beginner plan with straight forward recipes and simple cooking but each meal is designed with the children in mind. For a family chef, it is critical that the kids like what is being cooked and we made sure this plan wouldn't disappoint.
