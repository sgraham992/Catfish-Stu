Learn to create

Great Southern Food

Simply & Easily

revolutionize your cooking experience

We are teaming up with your local Lowe's Foods grocery store and our friend and incredible chef Daniel Heard to change the way you buy and consume food and teach you how to cook along the way.

Simply sign up for one of our available meals plans and each week you will pickup up your prepackaged food from your local Lowe's grocery store and receive a custom menu. The recipes are fully interactive with videos of a professional chef teaching you how to cook while making it simple and fun to create a southern, home-cooked meal!

Each night when you get home, open our app or website to login and start cooking. Your interactive recipes will help you layout and prepare everything you need and walk you through each step of the actual cooking process. Our recipes are designed to teach you how to cook, not just follow directions like a child. You will learn to prepare classic southern dishes with a modern, healthy twist to impress your family and friends.

Do more than just learn to cook

You don't just get recipes through Catfish Stu. In the South we also love a good cocktail. But no one likes the gross sugary premaid mixers you can buy at the store. Learn to make one signature cocktail from scratch each week. We promise you'll love how they turn out and you'll really impress your friends with this one.

No barbecue is complete without a signature punch for the group. No tailgate is right unless there's a real Bloody Mary to sip on. And no taco night is any fun without a margarita made like it should be.
