How to get started

We have made it incredibly quick and easy to transform the way you buy and cook your groceries. Sign up for one of our three meals plans and designate the number of people you want to feed each night.

Now each week you will receive a custom meal plan with interactive recipes and full shopping lists. But instead of walking around the grocery store for hours trying to find the items, we have teamed up with Lowe's Foods to make buying your groceries the easiest part.

Simply decide when you would like to pick up your groceries, add in any extra household items you might need, and let Lowe's pull together each and every ingredient just for you. Pick it up and don't go back to the grocery store all week because you forgot something!

Now just open our interactive app or members area of the website each night when you are ready to cook. Chef Daniel Heard will walk you step by step through each part of the recipe. He makes it simple and fun to cook and teaches you why you are actually doing what you're doing. You'll be impressing friends and family from day one!
